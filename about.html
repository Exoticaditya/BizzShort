Company Policies

Privacy Policy

At BizzShort, we take your privacy seriously. We collect and use personal information only to provide better services, personalized content, and improve user experience. We never sell your data to third parties.

Data Collection: We collect basic information such as name, email, browsing behavior, and preferences to deliver relevant content and advertisements.

Data Protection: All data is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with GDPR and Indian data protection regulations.

Terms of Service

By accessing BizzShort, you agree to our terms of service. All content is provided for informational purposes only and should not be considered financial or investment advice.

Content Usage: All articles, images, and multimedia content are copyrighted. Reproduction without permission is prohibited.

User Conduct: Users must not post spam, offensive content, or engage in activities that violate our community guidelines.

Editorial Policy

BizzShort maintains strict editorial independence. Our journalists and editors follow rigorous fact-checking procedures and ethical guidelines.

Accuracy: We verify all facts through multiple sources and make corrections promptly when errors are identified.

Transparency: Sponsored content and advertisements are clearly labeled. Our editorial content is never influenced by advertisers.

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and deliver personalized content.

Types of Cookies: Essential cookies (required for site functionality), analytics cookies (track usage patterns), and advertising cookies (deliver relevant ads).

Managing Cookies: You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.
