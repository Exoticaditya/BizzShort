YouTube to Article Converter

Paste a YouTube link to automatically create an article with embedded video

Example: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fH8Ir7doWGk

Article Preview

How to Use

  1. Copy the YouTube video URL from your browser
  2. Paste it in the input field above
  3. Optionally customize the title, category, and author
  4. Click "Convert to Article"
  5. Preview the generated article
  6. Click "Publish to Website" to add it to your site
Tips:
  • The video will be automatically embedded in the article
  • Article content is generated based on video metadata
  • Each article gets a unique ID for easy management
  • Published articles appear immediately in Latest Updates
